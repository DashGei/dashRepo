Reap 2nd-level Necromancy [CLE] [SBP] [LOK] [WIZ]

Casting Time: Instantaneous

Range: 60 feet

Components: V S M (a vial of ashes from a humanoid)

An arcane symbol appears and saps the life from a point of your choice in range. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a Constitution saving throw. A creature takes 2d12 necrotic damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. If a creature is reduced to 0 hit points by this spell it dies and the caster regains 1d4 hit points for each dead creature. A creature with no soul is unaffected by this spell. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher the damage increases by 1d12 and 1d4 respectively for each slot level above 2nd.
