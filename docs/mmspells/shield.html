Shield 1st-level Abjuration [SBP] [WIZ]

Casting Time: Reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack roll or targeted by the Magic Missile spell

Range: Self

Components: V S

Duration: 1 round

An imperceptible barrier of magical force protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a bonus to AC equal to your Mental Modifier, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from Magic Missile.
