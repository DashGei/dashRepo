# Commoner
*Medium Humanoid (Drow), Lawful Evil*

**Armor Class** 16  
**Hit Points** 50  
**Speed** 30 ft.
**Phys DC** 13
**Mental DC** 12

| STR   | DEX | WIS | INT | CHA   |
| ----- | --- | --- | --- | ----- |
| +3/+5 | +2  | +1  | +1  | +2/+4 |

### Skills
Athletics +5, Intimidation +4, Perception +3, Stealth +4

### Senses
Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 13

### Languages
Common, Elvish, Undercommon

---

### Traits

**Lolthsworn Venom.** When the Drow Warrior deals damage to a Poisoned creature, it deals an additional 1d4 Psychic damage.

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** The creature loses their Darkvision and has disadvantage on ability checks while in direct sunlight.

---

### Actions

**Strike.** Make an attack.

**Cleave.** Make an attack. Another creature within 5 feet of the target takes the same damage.

**Topple.** Make an attack. On a hit, a target with a lower Strength Modifier than the monster's largest modifier falls prone.

---

### Equipment

**Glaive.** *Melee:* +5 to hit, 10 ft reach.
**Hit:** 1d10 + 3 Physical


---

### Role
**Brute.** The Drow Warrior is designed to pressure the front line and capitalize on Poisoned enemies. It relies on allied Drow to apply conditions before moving in to finish weakened targets.

**Drow Warrior** Drow warriors are feared among the people of the underdark and surface alike. These soldiers often rise to their rank after a multitude of raids and are seasoned warriors who understand the threat of spellcasters and the benefits of focus fire on single targets. Drow foot soldiers look to them for orders.
