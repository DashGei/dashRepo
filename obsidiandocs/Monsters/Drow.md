# Drow Warrior
*Medium Humanoid (Drow), Lawful Evil*

**Armor Class** 16  
**Hit Points** 50  
**Speed** 30 ft.
**Phys DC** 13
**Mental DC** 12

| STR   | DEX | WIS | INT | CHA   |
| ----- | --- | --- | --- | ----- |
| +3/+5 | +2  | +1  | +1  | +2/+4 |

### Skills
Athletics +5, Intimidation +4, Perception +3, Stealth +4

### Senses
Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 13

### Languages
Common, Elvish, Undercommon

---

### Traits

**Lolthsworn Venom.** When the Drow Warrior deals damage to a Poisoned creature, it deals an additional 1d4 Psychic damage.

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** The creature loses their Darkvision and has disadvantage on ability checks while in direct sunlight.

---

### Actions

**Strike.** Make an attack.

**Cleave.** Make an attack. Another creature within 5 feet of the target takes the same damage.

**Topple.** Make an attack. On a hit, a target with a lower Strength Modifier than the monster's largest modifier falls prone.

---

### Equipment

**Glaive.** *Melee:* +5 to hit, 10 ft reach.
**Hit:** 1d10 + 3 Physical


---

### Role
**Brute.** The Drow Warrior is designed to pressure the front line and capitalize on Poisoned enemies. It relies on allied Drow to apply conditions before moving in to finish weakened targets.

**Drow Warrior** Drow warriors are feared among the people of the underdark and surface alike. These soldiers often rise to their rank after a multitude of raids and are seasoned warriors who understand the threat of spellcasters and the benefits of focus fire on single targets. Drow foot soldiers look to them for orders.

Common, Elvish, Undercommon

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** The creature loses their Darkvision and has disadvantage on ability checks while in direct sunlight.

**Skirmisher.** Drow Scouts apply pressure from the edges of battle, harassing vulnerable targets and spreading poison for the rest of the warband to exploit.

**Drow Scout.** Scouts serve as hunters, outriders, and pathfinders for Drow expeditions. Their ability to strike quickly and disappear into darkness makes them feared opponents both above and below ground.

Common, Elvish, Undercommon

---

### Traits

**Night Crawler.** The creature is invisible to creatures which rely on Darkvision to see it in Darkness.

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** The creature loses their Darkvision and has disadvantage on ability checks while in direct sunlight.

**Lolthsworn Poisons.** A creature Poisoned by an ability or action suffers the effects of the Frightened condition instead of the Poisoned condition. This ignores a creature's immunity or resistance to the Frightened condition.

---

**Shield:** Included in Armor Class.

---

**Drow Skulkers.** The backbone of most Drow raiding parties. Skulkers are expected to obey orders without hesitation and are drilled extensively in ambush tactics from a young age.

### Traits

---

### Traits

**Sunlight Sensitivity.** The creature loses its Darkvision and has disadvantage on ability checks while in direct sunlight.

---

**Strike.** Make an attack.

**Sting.** Make an attack. On a hit, the target becomes Poisoned until the end of its next turn.

### Spellcasting

The Drow Mage can cast the following spells at will:

**Cantrips** (1 Action)
- Acid Splash
- Mage Hand
- Minor Illusion

**Offense and Defense** (2 Actions)
- Witch Bolt
- Ray of Sickness
- Phantasmal Force

**Utility and Control** (2 Actions)
- Spider Climb
- Darkness
- Alarm

**Charged Action** (3 Actions)
- Web

---

### Role

**Controller.** Drow Mages manipulate the battlefield, restrict movement, and create opportunities for allied Drow to overwhelm isolated enemies.

**Drow Mage.** Drow Mages are entrusted with both magical and political authority within their warbands. Their mastery of poison and fear spells makes them invaluable during raids and ambushes.
