# Leadership Dice: 1

You possess Leadership Dice which represent your tactical advantage in combat. These dice start as d6, and increase in size as according to the Warlord features table.

You can hold a maximum number of Leadership Dice equal to your proficiency bonus, and these dice are lost when you take a long or short rest.

When a creature within 30 feet of you hits a target with an attack roll, you can spend one Leadership Die and add it to their damage roll.

# Tactical Orders: 1

As an action, you issue a Tactical Order to a creature within 60 feet that can see or hear you. The Tactical Order lasts until the start of your next turn, and when the order's trigger occurs you gain one Leadership Die.

Below is the list of Tactical Orders.

### Hold the Line

The target gains the benefits of the Dodge action.

**Trigger:** An attack misses the target or they succeed a Dexterity saving throw.

### Reposition

The target gains the benefits of the Disengage action.

**Trigger:** The target moves at least 10 feet.

### Brace Yourselves

The target gains a bonus to two saving throws equal to your Mental Modifier. The saving throws they gain bonuses for are your Physical and Mental Modifiers.

**Trigger:** The target succeeds on any saving throw.

### Opportunistic Maneuvers

The target gains an additional reaction.

**Trigger:** The target takes a reaction.

# Advanced Tactics: 2

You know how to push your advantages in combat. On your turn you can declare an order as an action. You expend one Leadership die, that creature gains one of the following benefits and regains hit points equal to one roll of your Leadership Dice.


### Directed Strike

Once on their next turn, the target creature can take the Attack action as a free action.

### Martial Prowess

Until the end of their next turn, the target creature has access to all Martial Feats which you know.

### Regroup

The target creature can immediately move up to half their speed. When the target creature starts their next turn, one ally within 5 feet regains the same amount of hit points as the target creature did from the Advanced Tactics healing.

# Martial Feats: 2

You have trained in special techniques that give you an edge in combat and beyond. You gain access to martial feats, as described in the Martial Feats section. You may only use one Feat Slot per turn.

**Primary Slots.** You have 2 Primary Slots, which you expend to activate your feats. You regain all expended slots when you finish a short rest. Your number of Primary Slots increases as you gain levels, as shown in the Feats Progression table.

**Feats Known.** You know 2 martial feats of your choice from your feat list. The Feats Known column of the Feats Progression table shows when you learn more feats. Additionally, when you gain a level in this class, you can replace one feat you know with another feat of your choice from your class feat list.

**Feat Tier.** You can learn feats of Tier 1 at level 2. At higher levels you gain access to higher tiers, as shown in the table.

# Fatigue Resistance: 5

Taking the Attack action does not impose penalties from the Fatigued condition.

---
# Inspiring Speech: 6

When you finish a short rest, all allies reduce their Exhaustion levels by 1 and regain additional hit points equal to two rolls of your Leadership Dice.

---

# Advanced Leadership: 9

When a Leadership Die is rolled, if the result is lower than your Mental Modifier, then that number is used instead of the roll.

---

# Unbreakable Will: 10

You have advantage on saving throws against the charmed and frightened conditions.

---
# Improved Orders: 11

When you use Hold the Line or Reposition, the target creatures speed is increased by 10 feet while the order is active.

Additionally, when you restore hit points to a creature with a Warlord feature, the creature also regains hit points equal to your Mental Modifier.

---

# Battlefield Intuition: 13

Once per long rest, before making a skill check with a proficient skill, you may treat the d20 roll as equal to your Warlord level.

---

# Shift the Field: 15

When you use Tactical Order, you can expend one Leadership Die to target an additional creature.

---

# Advanced Leadership II: 17

When you have no Leadership Dice to spend, you can fuel your features using d4 Leadership dice instead.

# Tireless Leader: 18

When you roll initiative, you gain two Leadership Dice.
