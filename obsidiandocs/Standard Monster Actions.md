Cleave - Make an attack against a creature, another creature within 5 feet of that creature also takes that damage.

Strike - Make an attack against a creature.

Brawl - Make an attack , that creature becomes grappled until they take an action to escape DC X.

Shove - Make an attack, that creature may then be moved up to 15 feet away.

Sting - Make an attack, that creature becomes poisoned until X.

Topple - Make an attack, that creature falls prone.

Expose - Make an attack, all allied attacks against that creature have advantage until the start of their turn.

Nimble - Take the Disengage action as one action.

(For reference)

Sweet Spot | When a creature ends their turn within 30-40 feet from you, you can use your reaction to activate this feat and make a ranged weapon attack against the creature.

Riposte | When a creature misses you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction to activate this feat and make one attack against that creature.

Interception | When a creature within 5 feet of you is hit with an attack roll, you can take a Reaction to reduce the damage dealt to the target by PB x 1d6. You must have be holding a melee weapon, shield, or have heavy armor equipped to use this feat.
