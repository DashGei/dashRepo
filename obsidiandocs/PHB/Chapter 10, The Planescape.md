Before the Planescape there was less than nothing, not even a hungering void to represent the nothingness that wasn't there. 

Weaved out of a dream from something far beyond the stars that were about to be birthed, was the Planescape.

___
**The Planescape** is a self contained planetary system with a total of 11 celestial bodies, each called a plane, or sometimes dimension. 

## Sol and Ea'or

Originally a singular star until the gods arrived to the Planescape, the twin suns Sol and Ego sit at the center of the Planescape giving energy to the world.

## Hell

Hell refers to the dismal plane of brimstone and storm which is ruled by Lucifer and houses the Devils who torture the people who break his deal. 

Hell is split into two parts, the mountainous canyons in which sit pools of boiling magma, and the salt flats which forever house roiling storms and tempests. 

## The Abyss

The Abyss is the infinite, mind-breaking labyrinth beneath Hell's surface. The chaotic Demons who dwell within often surface to make combat with their Devil enemies, usually just for the fun of it.
