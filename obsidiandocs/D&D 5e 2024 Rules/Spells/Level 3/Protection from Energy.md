#### Protection from Energy
*Level 3 Abjuration (Cleric, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], up to 1 hour

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has [[Rules/Common Terms/Resistance|Resistance]] to one damage type of your choice: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.
