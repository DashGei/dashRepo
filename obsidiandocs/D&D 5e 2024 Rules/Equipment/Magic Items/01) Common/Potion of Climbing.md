#### Potion of Climbing [[Potion of Climbing|(50 GP)]]
*Potion, Common*

**Attunement:** No

When you drink this potion, you gain a [[Climbing|Climb]] Speed equal to your [[Speed]] for 1 hour. During this time, you have [[Advantage]] on Strength ([[Athletics]]) checks to climb. This potion is separated into brown, silver, and gray layers resembling bands of stone. Shaking the bottle fails to mix the colors
