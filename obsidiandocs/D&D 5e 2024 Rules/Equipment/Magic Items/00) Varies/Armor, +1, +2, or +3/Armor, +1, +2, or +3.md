#### Armor, +1, +2, or +3 [[Armor, +1, +2, or +3|(Varies)]]
*Armor (Any Light, Medium, or Heavy), Varies*

**Attunement:** No

You have a bonus to Armor Class while wearing this armor. The bonus is determined by its rarity.

| Rarity                                   | Bonus |
| ---------------------------------------- | :---: |
| Rare  |  +1   |
| Very Rare      |  +2   |
| Legendary |  +3   |
