#### Potion of Healing (Greater) [[Potion of Healing (Greater)|(200 GP)]]
*Potion, Uncommon*

**Attunement:** No **Weight:** 0.5 lb

As a Bonus Action, you can drink it or administer it to another creature within 5 feet of yourself. The creature that drinks the magical red fluid in this vial regains 4d4 + 4 [[Hit Points]]. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
