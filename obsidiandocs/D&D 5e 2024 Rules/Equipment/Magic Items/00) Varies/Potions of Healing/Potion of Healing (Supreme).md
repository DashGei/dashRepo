#### Potion of Healing (Supreme) [[Potion of Healing (Supreme)|(20,000 GP)]]
*Potion, Very Rare*

**Attunement:** No **Weight:** 0.5 lb

As a Bonus Action, you can drink it or administer it to another creature within 5 feet of yourself. The creature that drinks the magical red fluid in this vial regains 10d4 + 20 [[Hit Points]]. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
