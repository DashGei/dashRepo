#### Potion of Healing [[Potion of Healing|(50 GP)]]
*Potion, Common*

**Attunement:** No **Weight:** 0.5 lb

As a Bonus Action, you can drink it or administer it to another creature within 5 feet of yourself. The creature that drinks the magical red fluid in this vial regains 2d4 + 2[[Hit Points]]. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
