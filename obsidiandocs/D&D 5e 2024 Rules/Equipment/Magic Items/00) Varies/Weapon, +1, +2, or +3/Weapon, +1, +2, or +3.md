#### Weapon, +1, +2, or +3 [[Weapon, +1, +2, or +3|(Varies)]]
*Armor (Any Simple or Martial), Varies*

**Attunement:** No

You have a bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The bonus is determined by its rarity.

| Rarity                                   | Bonus |
| ---------------------------------------- | :---: |
| Uncommon  |  +1   |
| Rare      |  +2   |
| Very Rare |  +3   |
