#### Mithral Ring Mail [[Mithral Ring Mail|(430 GP)]]
*Armor ([[Ring Mail]]), Uncommon*

**Attunement:** No

This armor weighs half as much as it normally would and can be worn under regular clothes. In addition, if the armor normally imposes [[Disadvantage]] on Dexterity ([[Stealth]]) checks or has a Strength requirement, the mithral version of the armor does not.
