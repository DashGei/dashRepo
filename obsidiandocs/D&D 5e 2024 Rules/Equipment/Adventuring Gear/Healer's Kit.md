#### Healer's Kit [[Healer's Kit|(5 GP)]] 
A Healer’s Kit has ten uses. As a [[Utilize]] action, you can expend one of its uses to [[Stable|stabilize]] an [[Unconscious]] creature that has 0 [[Hit Points]] without needing to make a Wisdom ([[Medicine]]) check.

**Weight:** 3 lb.
