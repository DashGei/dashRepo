A creature is Stable if it has 0 Hit Points but isn’t required to make [[Death Saving Throw|Death Saving Throws]], but it still has the [[Unconscious]] condition. If the creature takes damage, it stops being Stable and starts making Death Saving Throws again. A Stable creature that isn't healed regains 1 [[Hit Points|Hit Point]] after 1d4 hours.

You can take the [[Help]] action to try to stabilize a creature with 0 Hit Points, which requires a successful DC 10 Wisdom ([[Medicine]]) check.
