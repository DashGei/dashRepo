#### Draconic Resilience
The magic in your body manifests physical traits of your draconic gift. Your [[Hit Points|Hit Point]] maximum increases by 3, and it increases by 1 whenever you gain another Sorcerer level. Parts of you are also covered by dragon-like scales. While you aren’t wearing armor, your base Armor Class equals 10 plus your Dexterity and Charisma modifiers.
