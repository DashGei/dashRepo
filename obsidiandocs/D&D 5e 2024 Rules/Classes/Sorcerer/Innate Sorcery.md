#### Innate Sorcery
An event in your past left an indelible mark on you, infusing you with simmering magic. As a Bonus Action, you can unleash that magic for 1 minute, during which you gain the following benefits:
- The spell save DC of your Sorcerer spells increases by 1.
- You have [[Advantage]] on the attack rolls of Sorcerer spells you cast.
You can use this feature twice, and you regain all expended uses of it when you finish a [[Long Rest]].

***Sorcery Incarnate.*** At Sorcerer level 7, if you have no uses of Innate Sorcery left, you can use it if you spend 2 Sorcery Points when you take the Bonus Action to activate it. In addition, while your Innate Sorcery feature is active, you can use up to two of your Metamagic options on each spell you cast.

***Arcane Apotheosis.*** At Sorcerer level 20, while your Innate Sorcery feature is active, you can use one Metamagic option on each of your turns without spending Sorcery Points on it.
