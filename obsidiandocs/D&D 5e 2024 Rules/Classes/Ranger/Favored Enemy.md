#### Favored Enemy
You always have the *[[Hunter's Mark]]* spell prepared. You can cast it twice without expending a spell slot, and you regain all expended uses of this ability when you finish a [[Long Rest]].

The number of times you can cast the spell without a spell slot increases when you reach certain Ranger levels.

***Relentless Hunter.*** At Ranger level 13, taking damage can't break your [[Concentration]] on *Hunter's Mark*.

***Precise Hunter.*** At Ranger level 17, you have [[Advantage]] on attack rolls against the creature currently marked by your *Hunter's Mark*.

***Foe Slayer.*** At Ranger level 20, the damage die of your *Hunter's Mark* is a d10 rather than a d6
