#### Unarmored Movement
Your speed increases by 10 feet while you aren’t wearing armor or wielding a [[Equipment/Armor/Shield|Shield]]. This bonus increases when you reach certain Monk levels, as shown on the Monk Features table.

***Acrobatic Movement.*** At Monk level 9, while you aren’t wearing armor or wielding a Shield, you gain the ability to move along vertical surfaces and across liquids on your turn without falling during the movement.
