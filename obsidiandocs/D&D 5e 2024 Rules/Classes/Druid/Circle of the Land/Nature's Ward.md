#### Nature's Ward
You are immune to the [[Poisoned]] condition, and you have [[Rules/Common Terms/Resistance|Resistance]] to a damage type associated with your current land choice in the Circle Spells feature, as shown in the Nature’s Ward table.
###### Nature's Ward
| Land Type | Resistance |
| --------- | ---------- |
| Arid      | Fire       |
| Polar     | Cold       |
| Temperate | Lightning  |
| Tropical  | Poison     |
