#### Nature's Sanctuary
As a [[Magic]] action, you can expend a use of your [[Wild Shape]] and cause spectral trees and vines to appear in a 15-foot [[Cube]] on the ground within 120 feet of yourself. They last there for 1 minute or until you have the [[Incapacitated]] condition or die. You and your allies have [[Half Cover]] while in that area, and your allies gain the current [[Rules/Common Terms/Resistance|Resistance]] of your [[Nature's Ward]] while there. As a Bonus Action, you can move the Cube up to 60 feet to ground within 120 feet of yourself.
