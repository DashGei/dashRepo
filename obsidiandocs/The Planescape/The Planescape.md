Before the Planescape there was less than nothing, not even a hungering void to represent the nothingness that wasn't there. 

Weaved out of a dream from something far beyond the stars that were about to be birthed, was the Planescape.

___
**The Planescape** is a self contained planetary system with a total of 11 celestial bodies, each called a plane, or sometimes dimension.

### Sol and Ea'or

Originally a singular star until the gods arrived to the Planescape, the twin suns Sol and Ego sit at the center of the Planescape giving energy to the world.
