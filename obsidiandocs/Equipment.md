### Support Weapons
Employed by more organized militaries around the world. Squad weapons are expensive pieces of equipment which require special training to use. A creature untrained in a squad weapon is unable to use any of the features labeled with the squad weapon tag. A player can use a long rest of downtime to become trained in the weapon.

## Ao

_The Primordial Titan of Air_

Ao is the elemental titan of air—the gentle breeze that fills a child's kite, the roaring gale that strips mountains bare, and every current between. Unlike Leviathan's silent depth or Kaori's wounded stillness, Ao is carefree, curious, and utterly indifferent to mortal consequence. Ao has fleeting memory, and the past actions of the gods bear no power over his actions

**Domains:** Air, wind, sky, sound, freedom, caprice  
**Worship Method:** Ideals (defending the open sky from those who would chain it, and celebrating freedom in all its forms)

### Favor Criteria

- The party destroys or disables a device or spell designed to control the weather or bind air elementals.
    
- They free a flying creature from captivity.
    
- The party plays a harmless prank.
    
- The party defeats a creature that imprisons others.

### Disfavor Criteria

- The party is cruel to or harms a flying beast.
    
- They aid in the construction or maintenance of a structure that magically suppresses weather or natural wind.
    
- They mock the idea of freedom or call the carefree foolish.
    
- The party clips the wings of a captured enemy, literal or metaphorical, when they could have shown mercy.

### Boons

- **Minor Boon (FP 3–5): Tailwind.** When a party member, ally, or object falls from a height tall enough to deal fall damage, the DM rolls 1d6, on a 2-6 the spell Feather Fall is cast on the target.
    
- **Boon (FP 6–9): Whisper on the Wind.** Once per week (resetting on Prydei), the DM may allow the party to send a message as if by the _Sending_ spell, carried on a gust of wind to any creature they have met. The reply arrives within 1 minute as a soft breeze that speaks directly into the recipient's ear. Additionally, the DM may use this boon to deliver a cryptic hint or rumor to the party from Ao's rambling consciousness.
    
- **Major Boon (FP 10): Unchained Ascent.** Once per long rest, a party member can cast the spell _Fly_ on up to three creatures without expending a spell slot. The flight is accompanied by a constant, playful tailwind that makes cloaks and hair stream dramatically. Additionally, while flying under this effect, a creature can use a reaction to reroll a failed Dexterity saving throw.

### Woes

- **Minor Woe (FP –3 to –4): Contrary Wind.** Once per day, when the party attempts overland travel, an inexplicable headwind rises against them. Their travel speed is halved for 1d4 hours, and ranged weapon attacks made during this time have disadvantage while outside.
    
- **Greater Woe (FP –5): Zephyr's Prank.** Once per week (resetting on Prydei), The DM casts the spells Wind Wall or Control Winds on the party
